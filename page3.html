Portland

After a two day stay in Kennebunkport, we were off to Portland! There is lots to do in Portland, here are the highlights:

Day 1:

We arrived Portland and checked into our Airbnb. The first thing we did was walk to the Eastern Promenade for the amazing views. Next, we hopped on a Mailboat tour which is a MUST! It takes you to the smaller islands off the Portland coast and some of the most amazing views.

Day 2:

Day 2 was all about Beer! Portland has so many amazing breweries. We started the day at Allgash for a tasting and definitely recommend! Then we went to Rising Tide which was our absolute favorite, then we went to Sea Dog brewing which had a great gift shop.

Day 3:

The last day we had to check out the historic Portland Head Light on Cape Elizabeth. It was absolutely amazing and a must see!
